The list of items you would need to make the recipe

  • A big ol' pack of Chicken
  • Good ol butter
  • Salt
  • A mean spice rub

Steps to make juicy chicken breasts

  1. Line up the baking tray with parchment paper
  2. Smear the spice rub on the Chicken
  3. Pre heat the oven, then put the chicken in the oven for 450 degrees for 20 minutes
