Scrambled Eggs!

This recipe is heavily inspired by Gordon Ramsay's recipes

The list of items you would need to make the recipe

  • 4 Eggs
  • Good ol butter
  • Salt
  • Creme Fraiche

The steps you need to follow

  1. Butter up the pan (Don't say it, don't say it)
  2. Crack open the eggs
  3. Use a spatule and mix up the eggs thoroughly
  4. Add a bit of creme fraiche at the end!
